Thesis

"Veterinary medicine collaboration platform for consultations"

Building a patient management software with a goal to provide enhanced collaboration between veterinary medicine practitioners.

Through shared access to patient records, greater cooperation between veterinarians is realised, resulting in improved patient care and bring up to date diagnostics to the forefront of practitioners daily procedures.

This study handles the task of conduction a survey among Estonian veterinary doctors about the current state of diagnostic consultations among veterinarians and the planned platform that would include the integration of patient management system with collaborative diagnostics for health records.

Based on this feedback and evaluations a functional prototype is implemented.
